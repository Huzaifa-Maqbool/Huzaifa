## 🧠 About Me

> *"Defending systems through detection, data, and discipline."*

I'm a cybersecurity analyst specializing in **SOC operations**, **threat detection engineering**, and **GRC compliance** — with a twist: I build ML models that catch what rules miss.

- 🔭 Worked across **3 live SOC environments** — monitoring, triaging, and investigating real incidents
- 🤖 Collected **204,094 real Windows security events** from a live Wazuh SIEM and trained ML models to detect **Living-Off-The-Land (LOTL)** attacks — achieved **96.11% accuracy** *(research paper under peer review)*
- 🛡️ Built correlation rules, SOAR playbooks, and Snort/Suricata IDS/IPS rules in production environments
- 📋 Applied **NIST SP 800-53, PCI-DSS, GDPR, and HIPAA** in real policy and audit contexts
- 🎯 Currently seeking: **SOC Analyst (L1)**, **Threat Detection**, or **GRC roles**
- 📍 Based in **Islamabad, Pakistan**
